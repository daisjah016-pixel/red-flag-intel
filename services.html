Investigative Services

Red Flag Intel — A Division of Lawson Investigations

Red Flag Intel provides professional investigative and intelligence services for private individuals. All services are conducted lawfully, ethically, and in accordance with applicable regulations.

Background Investigations

Verification of personal, professional, and public-record information to support informed decision-making.

Surveillance & Activity Documentation

Lawful, discreet observation and documentation conducted in compliance with applicable laws. No illegal recording or intrusion is performed.

Infidelity & Relationship Investigations

Fact-based documentation handled with discretion and sensitivity. Our role is to document observable activity, not to interpret or advise.

Digital & Open-Source Intelligence (OSINT)

Analysis of publicly available online information, digital footprints, and open-source data. No hacking or unauthorized access is conducted.

Professional Standards

Outcomes are not guaranteed. Investigations involve variables beyond our control. Our obligation is to conduct lawful, ethical, and professional investigative work and report findings accurately.
